# AliExpress Affiliate Program Service Agreement

## Source

- [AliExpress Affiliate Program Service Agreement](https://terms.alicdn.com/legal-agreement/terms/suit_bu1_aliexpress/suit_bu1_aliexpress202003132026_84536.html)
- Checked: 2026-04-24

## Sourced Facts

- The agreement defines fraud to include attempts to create sales, leads, or click-throughs using robots, frames, iframes, scripts, or manual refreshes for the purpose of creating commissions.
- Participant enrollment requires portal registration and acceptance of additional advertising rules, policies, and terms that may be updated.
- AliExpress tracking records are the basis for calculating payable qualifying amounts unless otherwise specified.
- Participants must not interfere with the tracking system.
- Participants are responsible for their site/media, legal compliance, security, privacy, and non-infringement.
- Participants must submit required information including name, country, site/media information, and contact information. Inaccurate or incomplete information can lead to suspension of remittance, future accrual, API access, withdrawals, or other account functions.
- AliExpress states that it may not screen or verify all seller content and that participants must ensure hosting, placement, publishing, or distribution is lawful, good-faith, non-misleading, and non-fraudulent.

## AgentCart Interpretation

- Contributor profiles are not optional for AliExpress-style networks. Store contributor identity, country, registered media/site, contact, and network account proof where permitted.
- Do not present commission as guaranteed; tracking, refunds, invalidated links, violations, and policy changes can affect settlement.
- Treat robotic clicks, scripted opens, and frame/iframe use as hard-blocked.
- Store policy acceptance timestamp and the contributor's declared approved media URL.
